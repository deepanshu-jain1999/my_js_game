RULES OF THE GAME

  • USE ARROW KEYS TO MOVE SNAKE "UP DOWN LEFT RIGHT"
  • EVERY OBSTACLE POSSESS NUMBER THAT DECREASE THE LENGHT OF SNAKE ACCORDING TO THAT NUMBER , IF SNAKE COLLIDES
  • IF LENGHT OF SNAKE BECOME EQUAL TO OR LESS THAN ZERO OR SNAKE TOUCH BOUNDRY OF FIELD THEN GAME WILL GET OVER
  • GAME COMPRISES OF 2 LEVELS: LEVEL 1 AND LEVEL 2
